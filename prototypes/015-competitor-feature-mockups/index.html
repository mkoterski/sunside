Competitor features, folded into the SunSide design system

Four mockups that take the verified, useful features from ShadeSide, Sunseat and Sit In Shade and rebuild them with SunSide's own tokens, voice and the route-spine semantics from the design brief — rather than bolting on a foreign UI. Each screen extends an existing prototype component. The amber = sun-hits / indigo = shade / grey = no-clear-side mapping holds everywhere.
